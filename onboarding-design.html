Design decisions

Typography
Lora serif + DM Sans — matched to design kit
Lora (500) for headings and body prose — reads like a journal page. DM Sans (300–500) for UI chrome: labels, step indicators, buttons. This matches the design kit exactly; the mobile app uses Cormorant Garamond + Nunito — the kit moves to warmer, slightly more contemporary serif.
Goal → Reveal flow
Select goal → ambient tint shift → reveal screen
Hovering a goal warms the background with its bgTint. Clicking immediately slides to the Reveal screen, which shows the goal's personalised message ("A place to exhale.", "You are safe here.") with a radial glow in the accent colour and the goal's SVG glyph enlarged inside a soft ring.
Form steps (Name, Age, Country)
Minimal, unhurried — same register as the rest of the app
Each form step uses Lora italic for the subtitle ("helps us understand who uses DreamLog"). Inputs are transparent with a 1px border. The footer CTA toggles between "Continue" and "Skip" depending on whether data has been entered — no friction, no pressure.
Onboarding flow
7 screens · all optional past goal selection
Welcome → Goal → Reveal → Name → Age range → Country → Journal or Therapy. Goal is the only required choice (it sets the app theme and personalises AI reflections). Every subsequent step has a Skip option. The gate at the end routes to either the record screen or the therapy persona picker.
Animations
Slide + stagger — no jarring cuts
Screens slide in from the right (translateX 20px) with opacity, slide out left on exit. Goal rows stagger in with a 55ms delay per item. The reveal illustration scales in with a spring curve. Emotion bars and hairlines grow from 0 width. All transitions stay under 500ms to feel responsive, not theatrical.
