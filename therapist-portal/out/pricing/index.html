Common questions

Does it auto-renew?

No. Every plan is a 30-day pass. When it expires you return to Free automatically. Buy again if you want to continue.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, any time. Buy a new plan in-app and it activates immediately. Unused days on your current pass are not refunded.

Is the free plan actually free?

Yes, genuinely. 10 entries a month, full AI reflection, 7-day mood chart, crisis detection. No card required.

What currencies do you accept?

Prices are shown in INR for India, USD for most countries. Payment is processed by Stripe or in-app purchase depending on your platform.

Is Therapy Mode safe?

Crisis detection runs on every message. If distress is flagged, you get hotline resources immediately and the session ends safely.

Can a therapist access my data?

Only if you share it. You generate a passcode-protected link. It expires in 72 hours. Your therapist sees mood trends and AI summaries — never raw recordings.
