Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What DreamLog Actually Is

DreamLog is a voice journaling app. You talk, we turn it into text, reflect it back to you, and help you make sense of your inner life over time. The whole thing is built on one core belief: your thoughts belong to you. Not us. Not brands. Not data brokers. You.

This document tells you exactly what we collect, why we need it, how long we hold onto it, and what you can do about any of it.

What We Collect and Why

Voice recordings

When you record something, the audio goes straight to secure cloud storage without ever touching our servers. Once it has been transcribed, the audio file is deleted permanently. We genuinely don't keep it. There's no archive of your voice sitting somewhere.

Transcripts

The text version of what you said is stored on our servers. This is the heart of DreamLog - it's what powers your journal history, your reflections, and the emotional patterns we track over time.

AI-generated content

The reflections, mood scores, emotional tags, key quotes, nudge messages, and dream interpretations we generate - these get saved alongside your entry so your history stays intact.

Your account details

Just your email and display name. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we only see what you explicitly authorise - nothing more.

Your notification token

If you turn on morning nudges, we store a device token to deliver them. You can switch it off anytime in Settings and it's gone.

Basic usage data

Things like when you recorded, how long the session was, and which version of the app you're using. We don't track what you tap, how you scroll, or anything behavioural beyond the app itself.

What We Don't Collect

No location. No contacts. No photos. No tracking pixels. No advertising SDKs quietly hoovering up your data in the background. We don't sell your data for advertising - not now, not ever.

How We Actually Use Your Data

To write your reflection

Your latest transcript, along with your five most recent entries for context, gets sent to Anthropic's Claude API. That's what generates your personalised reflection. Anthropic doesn't use API data for training by default.

To spot if you're struggling

Every transcript is automatically checked for signs of distress - first by keyword matching, and if it's unclear, by a follow-up AI check. If something concerning is detected, crisis resources appear immediately. No human reads your entries.

To get better over time

The more you use DreamLog, the more it understands your recurring patterns, themes, and emotional language. This all lives in your account and never leaves it.

To send your morning nudge

If you opt into notifications, we craft a short message based on your last entry and send it at whatever time you choose via Firebase. It's entirely optional.

For Therapist Share

If you choose to share a link with your therapist, they get an anonymised view of your mood trends and AI summaries - never your actual transcripts or recordings. The link is passcode-protected and expires after 72 hours automatically.

How Long We Keep Things

Audio recordings

Deleted immediately after transcription - not recoverable.

Transcripts & analyses

Until you delete the entry or your account.

Share links

72 hours, then permanently removed.

Account data

Until you request deletion.

The Services We Work With

We use a small number of trusted external providers to run DreamLog. Here's who they are and what they do with your data:

Anthropic (Claude) ↗

Generates your reflections and handles crisis detection. They don't train on API submissions.

OpenAI (Whisper) ↗

Transcribes your audio. Audio is sent to them only for this purpose.

Cloudflare R2

Holds your audio temporarily while transcription is happening, then it's deleted.

Supabase ↗

Manages your account login, including social sign-in.

Firebase (Google)

Delivers your push notifications if you've opted in.

Azure Speech Services ↗

If you use Therapy Mode with AI voice, your assistant's responses are processed here.

Stripe ↗

Handles all payments. We never see your card details - Stripe takes care of that entirely.

A Note on Mental Health Data

Journal entries and mood data are sensitive. We treat them that way:

  • No DreamLog employee reads your entries.
  • Crisis detection is automated - no human review.
  • Therapist Share is opt-in, anonymised, and passcode-protected.
  • We don't use your emotional data to target you with ads.
  • We don't share it with insurers, employers, or anyone else.

Children

DreamLog isn't for anyone under 13. If you think a child has made an account, email us at support@dreamlog.app and we'll delete it straight away.

Your Rights

You're in control. Here's what you can do at any time:

  • Export your data - Settings → Export my data. You'll get a PDF of your full journal history.
  • Delete an entry - Tap and delete from the journal screen. It's gone permanently.
  • Delete your account - Settings → Delete account. Everything goes - entries, analyses, account data. This can't be undone.
  • Turn off notifications - Settings → Notifications, or through your phone's system settings.
  • Revoke therapist access - Share links expire on their own after 72 hours, or contact us to kill one early.

For a full copy of everything we hold about you, or for any GDPR requests, email support@dreamlog.app.

Security

All data moves over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed, never stored as plain text. Tokens expire. Audio is stored in private buckets with time-limited access only. Database access is locked down to our application servers only.

No system is completely airtight. If you find something, please let us know at support@dreamlog.app before making it public.

If This Policy Changes

If we make any significant changes, we'll let you know by email or in-app - at least 14 days before they kick in. The date at the top of this page always shows you what version you're reading.
